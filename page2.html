MOP FORGE is a creative tool that lets users design and customize their own alphabets using a wide variety of digital brushes.Powered by P5.js,the project provides an intuitive platform where anyone can experiment with brush styles,shapes,and textures to create unique letterforms.

Step 1:

Choose from a collection of painting brushes to craft your own letters and symbols.

Step 2:

Draw freely with responsive brushes and press "SAVE"to save your work.

Step 3:

Generate entire sets of alphabets, numbers, and punctuation.

Step 4:

Once you've designed your set of characters, press "EXPORT" to dowload them in PDF format.
